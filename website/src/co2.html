CO2 Hierarchy

The Eco-score® is an indicator representing the environmental impact of food products. It classifies products in 5 categories (A, B, C, D, E), from the lowest to the highest impact. The following bubbles represent the different food groups that we consume in our daily lives. They are classified from left to right. Left being the least polluting. Their color is associated with an eco score and is assigned to them according to the average CO2 they emit. Their size is linearly proportional to the number of products in the category.
