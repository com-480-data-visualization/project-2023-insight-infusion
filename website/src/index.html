Insight Infusion

The development of food engineering techniques coupled with ever-increasing globalization has made it possible to offer a greater variety of products around the world, all at low cost.

Nevertheless, these advances have many negative impacts on our health (diseases, obesity) and our environment (greenhouse gases, deforestation, pollution, destruction of biodiversity).

The objective of our project is to inform consumers about the impacts of their food through vulgarized and high impact visualizations and explanations.
